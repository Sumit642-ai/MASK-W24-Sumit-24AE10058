Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is a dark fantasy manga and anime series about a world of sorcerers who exorcise curses. The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes possessed by a powerful cursed spirit after swallowing a cursed talisman

One Piece

One Piece is a Japanese manga series and TV show about a young pirate captain and his crew who search for the mythical treasure known as the "One Piece"

Naruto

Naruto is a Japanese manga series and anime television series about a young ninja named Naruto Uzumaki who dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village
